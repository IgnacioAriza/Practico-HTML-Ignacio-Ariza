Epiphany

I was not expecting this.

You have stayed the hungry hunters,
You have locked Death’s door.
For all your

skulkling,

slinking,
sneering,
For all I was fearing;
I was not expecting this:
For you to step into the light
and reveal yourself.
I see you;
I see the lamb
you hide under the wolf’s skin.
